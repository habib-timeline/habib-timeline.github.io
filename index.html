Intracellular Delivery & RBC Mechanics
Microfluidic shear-mediated membrane deformation enables controlled encapsulation of functional proteins into erythrocytes — a platform for cell-based drug delivery. Validated experimentally and modeled with COMSOL FSI simulation.
Lab-on-a-Chip COMSOL FSI Drug Delivery US Patent

BioMEMS & Organelle Engineering
A lab-on-chip device exploiting differential membrane stiffness to selectively extract functional mitochondria — without centrifugation or detergents. Preserves mitochondrial membrane potential for downstream applications.
MEMS Fabrication Mitochondria Cell Lysis Microsystems

Cell Deformability & Image Analysis
Quantitative phase deformability cytometry for non-invasive, label-free cell characterization at high throughput. Extended with Deformax — a real-time RBC deformability analyzer with runtime-adjustable image processing and live DI computation.
